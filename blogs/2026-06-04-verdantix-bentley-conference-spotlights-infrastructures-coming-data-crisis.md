Verdantix analysts at Bentley Systems' Illuminate Berlin 2026 conference highlighted that Europe faces significant infrastructure challenges requiring 12 trillion euros in investment by 2040. The research emphasizes that fragmented data lost at project handover and siloed across disciplines represents the sector's most pressing unresolved issue, with digital twins, connected data platforms, and AI emerging as critical solutions.
