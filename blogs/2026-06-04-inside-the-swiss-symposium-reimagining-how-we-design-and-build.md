ETH Zurich's Future of Construction symposium showcased innovations including Tor Alva, the world's tallest 3D-printed structure. Key discussions centered on how robotics and AI are transforming construction, with emphasis on the need for open data systems and interoperability to enable AI agents to work effectively across siloed infrastructure projects.
